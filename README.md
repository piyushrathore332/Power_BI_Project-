# Power_BI_Project-
1. Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution. It allows stakeholders to identify key drivers of revenue and areas for service improvement across various outlet types and locations.

2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks several critical metrics to gauge business health:
+1


Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all grocery items.
+2


Average Sales: The mean revenue per transaction, helping to understand customer spending patterns.


Total Items Sold: A count of all individual products moved through the platform.
+1


Average Rating: Customer feedback score (e.g., 4.5/5), used to measure service and product quality.
+1
